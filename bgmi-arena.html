Mission Briefing

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before entering the arena.

◈ Still have questions?

Our support team is online 24/7 on Discord and WhatsApp.

Fill out the registration form with your team details and all 4 player UIDs. Pay the entry fee via UPI and send the payment screenshot to our WhatsApp. Your slot will be confirmed within 30 minutes.
Room ID and Password are shared exclusively in our Discord server exactly 10 minutes before the match starts. Make sure you're in the server and have notifications enabled. Late joiners will not receive Room IDs.
Prize money is sent via UPI (PhonePe/GPay/Paytm) within 24 hours of the tournament ending. Winners must submit their UPI ID after results are announced. Large amounts (₹10,000+) may take up to 48 hours for verification.
Refunds are issued if a tournament is cancelled by us. If your team no-shows or is disqualified for rule violations, no refund is provided. Refund requests must be made before the tournament starts.
Rankings are calculated as: Placement Points (based on your final position in each match) + Kill Points (1 point per kill). Season rankings are the cumulative total across all matches played in that season.
Contact our admin on Discord immediately with screenshots and video evidence. Admins have the authority to replay a round if there's a verified server-side issue. Player-side issues (lag, crash) do not qualify for replays.
